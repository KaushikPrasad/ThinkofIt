ABOUT THINK OF IT FOUNDATION

"THINK OF IT" Foundation is a non profit youth-led like-minded community for young leaders to discover themselves via thought-leadership and meaningful nation building activities through TOFI - Youth Leadership Council.

"THINK OF IT" intends to direct the attention of the youth towards issues that need immediate attention and implementation in terms of services such as interning in govenment school where conditions are wrecked beyond repair,to share their knowledge with children who could very well be leaders of tomorrow themselves and to lead a nation that upholds values, hard-work and determination.

Working with young minds to create societal change, awareness about contemporary issues and policies and build life-long relations, TOFI - YLC aims to revolutionize traditional leadership definition and create leaders with TOFI impact model and regulatory council.
